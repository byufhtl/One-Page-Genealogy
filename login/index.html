Created at BYU

One Page Genealogy was developed on the Brigham Young University campus. Here in the Family History Technology Lab we focus on developing innovative technologies to improve family history. Try out our other project at the lab website.

Display it All in Less Space

One Page Genealogy uses a space reduction algorithm to push branches of your tree together to create a compact chart without wasting space with incomplete branches. One Page Genealogy allows you to print a visually appealing chart of your ancestors that you can print onto one page.

Partners

One Page Genealogy is Family Search certified. This means we retrieve your family tree right from Family Search. When you find a problem in your tree you can go directly to Family Search to fix it.
