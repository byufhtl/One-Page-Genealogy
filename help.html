Getting Started

One Page Genealogy is the perfect site to create a compact tree chart of your family with a few quick clicks.

How to Print or save as a PDF

Here is the current way of creating a PDF that you can edit and make the size you desire:

  • Zoom out on the chart until your whole chart can be seen on your browser page.

  • Drag the chart over to be as close to the left side of the screen as possible.

 

If you are using Google Chrome:

  • Right click the chart and click "Print"

  • If you want a PDF, make sure the Destination is "Save as PDF." If it isn't, just click "Change..." and select the "Save as PDF" option. This way, you can edit the chart in any PDF editor and make the chart more readable if needed.

 

If you are using Firefox:

  • At the top on the right of the browser click the icon with three horizontal bars.

  • Click print, then print again at the top of the new page that new pops up.

  • If you want a PDF, click "Print to File" at the top of the printer options. This way, you can edit the chart in any PDF editor and make the chart more readable if needed.

Download Chart as SVG

To download the current chart as an SVG click the downward pointing arrow icon at the top right of the page
